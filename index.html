关于昂美 About MATTRIO

MATTRIO 是一家专业提供汽车后市场咨询服务的顾问公司，致力于为各类零配件制造商 及贸易公司提供汽车后市场咨询、数据定制、样件采购和产品电子目录等一系列服务。

MATTRIO is a professional consulting company for automotive industry, providing series service of aftermarket consulting, data service, sample purchasing and electronic product catalogue, etc.

我们拥有一支汽车售后从业经验丰富的专业队伍，平均从业资历超过5年。

We owned a professional aftermarket consulting team, each member has over 5 years work experience in automotive aftermarket
